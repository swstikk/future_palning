## ⚠️ 3. COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **The "Collector" Fallacy:** Hoarding PDFs, roadmaps, and syllabi without actually executing them. You have excellent roadmaps; now you must write code and solve equations. **Action:** Measure progress by lines of code written and math problems solved, not videos watched.
2. **Ignoring the Biological Wet Lab Reality:** As a computational person, it's easy to think biology is just data. Remember that biological data is noisy, messy, and prone to batch effects. Always study the *source* of the data (how a sequencer works, how CRISPR actually cuts DNA).
3. **Burnout:** 3-4 hours daily alongside Class 11/12 is grueling. If you miss a day, do not try to do 8 hours the next day. Consistency over 2 years beats bursts of 10-hour days.
4. **Premature Optimization:** Do not worry about building your own Quantum ML algorithm from scratch yet. Learn to use existing libraries (PyTorch, Qiskit, Scanpy) masterfully first.
