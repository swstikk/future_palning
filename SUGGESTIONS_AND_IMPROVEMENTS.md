    *   *Fix:* Stick rigidly to the Math prerequisites outlined in your Project Prometheus.

    *   *Fix:* Focus on *mechanisms* and *computational representation*. Don't just memorize the 12 Hallmarks of Aging; think about how you would represent them mathematically or in a database.
4.  **Ignoring Hybrid Quantum-Classical Systems:** True quantum computers capable of simulating complex biological molecules are years away.
    *   *Fix:* Focus heavily on PyTorch/JAX and hybrid algorithms (like VQE) that use classical computers to do the heavy lifting, augmented by quantum processors.
