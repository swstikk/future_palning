## 🧠 PART 2: STRATEGIC EFFICIENCY & TIME MANAGEMENT

You are trying to master 5 highly difficult fields in parallel (Quantum, ML, Bio, Genetics, Algo Trading) within 3-4 hours a day. **Burnout is your biggest enemy.**

### 🛑 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Tutorial Hell:** Spending months watching ML or Bio videos without writing a single line of code or answering test questions.
2. **Context Switching Penalty:** Trying to do all 5 fields *every single day*. Your brain needs time to load context.
3. **Ignoring Foundational Math:** As your *Project Prometheus* blueprint correctly notes, failing to learn Linear Algebra means you will fail at Quantum Computing and ML.

* **The 2/1/1 Split:** Since you only have 3-4 hours, do not do all subjects daily. Group them.

Learn Time-Series ML (LSTMs, Transformers, Reinforcement Learning) using financial data (Algo Trading). This kills two birds with one stone.
* **Focus on the Intersection:** The fastest way to your ultimate goal (Biological Immortality research) is the intersection.
  * **ML + Bio** = Protein prediction (Boltz-1) and drug discovery.
  * **Quantum + Bio** = Simulating molecular interactions (VQE).
