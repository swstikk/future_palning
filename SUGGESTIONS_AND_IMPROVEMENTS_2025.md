## 3. ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Skipping Foundational Math for "Cool" Code**:
   - You cannot do Quantum Computing or advanced ML without Linear Algebra, Eigenvalues, and Tensor Calculus. Do not jump to Qiskit or PyTorch without mastering Module M1-M4.
2. **Ignoring Chemistry**:
   - You want to do Genetic Engineering and Bioinformatics, but biology *is* chemistry. Pay extra attention to the Biochemistry sections (1.4).
3. **Tutorial Hell**:
   - Following a YouTube tutorial on how to build a CNN is easy. Building a CNN from scratch to classify DNA sequences with no guide is hard. Do the hard thing.
4. **Burning Out**:
   - You are 16. The goal is biological immortality—it's a marathon, not a sprint. If you feel burned out, drop down to 1 hour of review a day and rest. Consistency over 5 years beats 12-hour days for a month followed by quitting.
